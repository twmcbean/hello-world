My Personal Page

On this page you will find a biography of the developer of this page, along with contact information.

I am a Software/Web developer in the making. I decided to embark on this journey to Software/Web developing for a better life for my family and I. This passion I have for technology started at a very young age, where i would tend to break cellphones, computers and then try to repair them; most of which were successful. My Grandfather who is an Electrical Contractor would bring me to work with him to wire houses and hotels, his boss saw me and invited us to his office to diagnose an internet connection issue he was having. Realizing i had the potential to resolve the issue my Grandfather gave me permission to fix his servers wired connection issue at the young age of only 12 years old. Thats when i truly found myself.

Journey to Web Developer

The journey has been quite interesting, it has opened my eyes to a whole new world. Learning all the different factors and languages that assist both front and back end to produce work of art for user experience is fascinating to me. Learning all the elements and attributes are challenging however, with the help of my TA Tobi, i know i will be successful in this journey.
